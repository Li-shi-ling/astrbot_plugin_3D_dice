Frequently Asked Questions

What dice types are available?

We support D4 (4-sided), D6 (standard 6-sided), D8 (8-sided), and D20 (20-sided) dice, perfect for D&D and other tabletop RPGs.

Does it work offline?

Yes! Roll My Dice is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Install it on your device and use it anywhere, even without internet.

Is it really free?

Absolutely free, no account required, no ads, no tracking. Just roll dice.

Can I use it for Dungeons & Dragons?

Yes! Our D20 and other polyhedral dice are perfect for D&D, Pathfinder, and any tabletop RPG.
